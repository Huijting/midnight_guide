# 🌙 WoW: Midnight Companion Guide

**v1.1** — A bilingual (NL/EN) Progressive Web App for World of Warcraft: Midnight.

**App build v3.6.14** (Mar 2026) — Delves: dynamic EU Bountiful day in the header; fallback + warning when daily JSON is stale; mobile key row without a blank Wowhead area; more reliable GitHub Actions for `prey-today` / `bountiful-today`. In-app: **About this app** → changelog and (once) the update banner.

**Live:** [huijting.github.io/midnight_guide/](https://huijting.github.io/midnight_guide/)

---

## 📍 Contents

### 🌙 Home
Landing page with navigation cards and direct links to all sections.

### 🏰 Raids
Raid boss guides with mechanics per role (Tank/Healer/DPS):
- **The Dreamrift** — Chimaerus, the Undreamt God (opens March 17, 2026)
- The Voidspire — 6 bosses *(coming later)*
- March on Quel'Danas — 2 bosses *(coming later)*

### ⚔️ Dungeons
All Season 1 M+ dungeons (8 total) with boss tactics, route tips and M+-specific advice. Plus 4 Normal-only dungeons.
Includes **Trash Mob Guide** in the Route tab with 69 mobs documented, color-coded tags (Interrupt/Dodge/Purge/CC/Tank).

### 🎯 Prey System (New in v1.0)
A complete, step-by-step guide for the new solo-friendly endgame activity. Includes unlock questline, difficulties and rewards.

### 💎 Delves (Improved in v1.1)
All 12 Midnight Delves with /way, loot table and key info. Click a Delve name for a short overview (3 tips). Tap **Full Guide** for a step-by-step walkthrough with events, boss mechanics and secrets — in Dutch and English.

### 📅 Weekly
Overview of weekly activities: World Bosses, Timewalking, Crafting Orders, Delves and more.

### 🔨 Professions
All 13 professions with trainer /way coordinates, a comprehensive leveling guide (1-100), KP Guide, and KP Sources.
**New in v1.0:** Character tracking! Check off KP treasures per character.

### ⚡ Affixes
Complete overview of all M+ affixes in Season 1 with explanations and tips:
- 🛡️ Lindormi's Guidance (+2–+4) — learning affix, no death penalty
- 💀 Xal'atath's Bargain (+5–+11) — 4 rotation variants: Ascendant, Voidbound, Pulsar, Devour
- ⚔️ Fortified / Tyrannical (+7) — alternating weekly
- 💥 Fortified + Tyrannical simultaneously (+10)
- ⏱️ Xal'atath's Guile (+12) — deaths cost 15 sec

### 🎯 Specs
All 13 classes, all specs — each with 9 tabs:
- ⚡ Cheat Sheet | 🔄 Rotation | 📊 Stats | ⏱️ Cooldowns
- 💡 Tips | ⌨️ Macros | 🎯 Resource | 🧪 Consumables
- 🌳 Talents — builds for M+, Raid, Delves and Leveling

### 📖 Glossary
WoW terms explained for beginners, including inline gold tooltips in dungeon tips.

## 📱 Install as app

Tap the install icon in your browser (Chrome/Safari) to add the app to your home screen. Works offline!
