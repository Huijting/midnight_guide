# What's New in v1.1 — 20 March 2026

## English
- **Delves:** Click any Delve name to see a short overview — what to do, what's dangerous, and a pro-tip.
- **Full Guide:** Tap the Full Guide button for a complete walkthrough. Events, boss mechanics and secrets — easy to read!
- **Two languages:** Dutch and English. Use the flags at the top to switch.
